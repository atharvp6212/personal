गवती चहा

Welcome to Shri Ganesh Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, PU. Ahilyadevi Adhyapak Mahavidyalaya Sangavi. Cymbopogon, or lemongrass or Gavati Chaha, is a tropical plant known for its lemony flavor and aroma, derived from citronella oil in its leaves. Used in cooking for its refreshing taste, lemongrass also offers potential health benefits, including digestive support and antioxidants. Its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties make it valuable in traditional medicine. With a pleasant fragrance, lemongrass is widely cultivated for culinary, medicinal, and ornamental purposes.
